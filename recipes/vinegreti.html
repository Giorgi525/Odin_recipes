This is Soviet christmas/New year special. dish so popular 15 countries used to make for 70 years

It is made using simple Ingredients which can be found at home almost any time

Ingredients:

  • Potatos
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • mayo
  • pickles
  • eggs

Lets get started

  1. Boil potatos , carrots and eggs
  2. slice pickles , boiled eggs , potatos and carrot
  3. combine them in big bowl
  4. add generous amounts of mayo
  5. add salt and peppers to taste
  6. let it cool down and enjoy
