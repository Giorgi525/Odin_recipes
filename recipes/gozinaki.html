Gozinaki is staple of Georgian New years sweets , its really easy to make and has crazy nutritional value

you can make this delicious treat in under 20 minutes , so lets get started

Ingredients:

  • nuts(walnuts or hazelnuts)
  • high quality honey

How to make it:

  1. dice nuts on cutting board
  2. roast nuts on a pan
  3. add honey to nuts after they turn light brown
  4. take mixture out on cutting board from pan
  5. slice while hot and wait to cool down
  6. enjoy
