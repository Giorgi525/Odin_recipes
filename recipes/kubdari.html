Kubdari

Home

this is a traditional Georgian dish called Kubdari which is really easy to make

It uses basic yeasted dough and chopped meat seasoned of course with peppers and Svanian salt

Ingredients:

  • dough
  • meat
  • svanian salt
  • butter
  • seasonings

steps

  1. finely chop the meat
  2. mix seasoning and small amount of butter
  3. mix seasoned butter with meat
  4. roll out the dough and put seasoned meat on top
  5. tuck in the dough and bake it
  6. after baking smear butter on top
  7. Enjoy
